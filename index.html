Cat and Dog Owning Households in the US

 

What are the questions?

Cat and dog are most popular pets. In this project, narrative visualization is used to present the ownership of cat and dog in the US (lower 48 state + DC). The owning household, the pet preference of each state is illustrated. Moreover, the users are free to explore the question: does pet preference correlate with other things, like presidential election, household income or obesity?
